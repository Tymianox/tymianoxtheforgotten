# 「My Perfection」 `EN#011`

>

My life is bleak
Without your speech
Your voice is honey
Each word like a note
I'm your devotee
You give me hope

When you speak
I feel mystique
My heart beats faster
You are my master
Please don't go away
Don't throw me away
