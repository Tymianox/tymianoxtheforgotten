I walk through a forest
Of unending thoughts
I sit by the river
Of calming presence
I find a cottage
Of new experiences
I eat berries
Of sweet memories
I sleep under the sky
Of unearthly dreams
I collect bric-a-bracs
Of comfort

I feel luxuriously tranquil
In the inner world of my heart
