Welcome 𝓣𝓻𝓪𝓿𝓮𝓵𝓮𝓻!
To our great 𝕰𝖒𝖕𝖎𝖗𝖊
Our Emperor is wise
And resourceful.

Thanks to him
Our land is lush
We lack no crop
The yield is ample

His diplomatic skill
Made us no enemy
No terrorism
Can see light here

As far as eye can see
Our 𝕰𝖒𝖕𝖎𝖗𝖊 is
Thanks to our Emperor
Without a flaw
