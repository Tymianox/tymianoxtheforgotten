#poetry/series/the-hayworth-tarot

The Vessel called 

He turned away

Cup King's gifts stood before him

He made his choice

He drunk thirstily 

His thirst yet to be sated

  

Aqua does not forget

Aqua does not forgive

Aqua always yearns for more
