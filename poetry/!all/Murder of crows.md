I saw them
Or... what used to be them
Sitting by the wall
In a - pool of blood

Legs crossed, head low
A hole in their chest
In their palms a heart
Brain dripping on it from their head

It was me, I killed them
And I don't remember why
And I won't repent
I also don't know why
