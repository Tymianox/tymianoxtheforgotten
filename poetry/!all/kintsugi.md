beat & battered
tired of this world
I survive somehow
without a plan in mind

I yearn for the end
but i hang on to live
it’s not hope
I don’t know what

but if I’ll get out alive
my cracks and scars
will glimmer with golden light
enhanced by the hardship
maybe that’s something to look forward to
