#poetry/series/the-hayworth-tarot

Embraced by flames

But left to live

A ghost of existance

Ashes of truth

One who forgets the past

  

Cup King calls him weak

Pentacle King calls him stagnant

Sword King calls him lost

But he listens only to the flames
