# 「Peace」 `EN#238`

---

>

A much needed break
From worrying about it all
Taking life slowly
Step by
Step
Day by
Day

Don't think about what comes
After, turn down the volume
In your head, stop what you're
Doing, enjoy the breeze
Enjoy the tease
