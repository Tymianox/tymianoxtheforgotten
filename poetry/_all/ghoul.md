no heart to study
even at gunpoint
i'd rather kill somebody
'cause there is no point

when i hear about school
i become lifeless
just tired, like a ghoul
my energy vanishes 

with no end in sight
my eyes on the floor
i take my mind off the fact
by wasting time some more
